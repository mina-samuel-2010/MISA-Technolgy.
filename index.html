About Me-

I’m Mina Samuel, a Egyptian programmer and designer with over five years of experience in web development and Android app programming. I also specialize in photo editing using Photoshop, logo design with Illustrator, and video production and editing with Premiere Pro. I am one of the founders of MISA Technology, an online platform established five years ago, where I played a key role in its creation. The platform includes several specialized magazines covering topics such as programming, photo editing, and video production.
